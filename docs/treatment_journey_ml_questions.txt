Treatment Journey ML Analysis Questions
=====================================

These questions can be analyzed using the treatment_journey_ml dataset to improve patient care and practice operations.

Treatment Journey Questions
-------------------------
1. What factors best predict treatment plan acceptance?
2. How does procedure complexity (duration, multi-visit) affect acceptance rates?
3. What is the typical time between treatment planning and completion?
4. How do seasonal patterns affect treatment acceptance?

Financial Behavior Questions
--------------------------
1. What factors predict payment within 30/60/90 days?
2. How does insurance status affect treatment acceptance?
3. How do existing balances impact new treatment acceptance?
4. What is the relationship between family financial history and individual payment behavior?
5. How do payment patterns vary by procedure category?

Communication Pattern Questions
-----------------------------
1. How does communication frequency affect treatment acceptance?
2. Which communication method (text, email, phone) is most effective?
3. What is the optimal timing for patient communications?
4. How does recent communication activity correlate with payment behavior?

Appointment Behavior Questions
----------------------------
1. How do missed appointments predict future treatment acceptance?
2. What is the relationship between appointment frequency and treatment completion?
3. How does family appointment history affect individual behavior?
4. What appointment patterns indicate risk of treatment plan abandonment?

Patient Demographics & History
----------------------------
1. How do age and gender affect treatment acceptance and completion?
2. What is the impact of family dental history on individual treatment decisions?
3. How does patient longevity with the practice affect treatment acceptance?
4. What patient characteristics predict long-term treatment success?

Family Influence Questions
------------------------
1. How does family procedure history influence individual treatment decisions?
2. What is the correlation between family missed appointments and individual behavior?
3. How do family payment patterns affect individual payment likelihood?
4. What is the impact of family size on treatment acceptance?

Financial Risk Assessment
-----------------------
1. What combination of factors indicates high risk for payment default?
2. How accurately can we predict insurance coverage utilization?
3. What patterns indicate potential financial barriers to treatment?
4. How do adjustments and insurance payments affect patient payment behavior?

Operational Insights
------------------
1. How does procedure duration affect scheduling and completion rates?
2. What is the relationship between provider time and treatment acceptance?
3. How do different procedure categories perform in terms of completion rates?
4. What scheduling patterns optimize treatment acceptance and completion?

Patient Engagement Metrics
------------------------
1. What communication patterns indicate high patient engagement?
2. How does engagement level correlate with treatment success?
3. What combination of factors predicts long-term patient retention?
4. How do different communication strategies affect patient outcomes?

Risk Stratification
-----------------
1. What factors identify high-risk patients for treatment abandonment?
2. How can we predict likelihood of missed appointments?
3. What patterns indicate potential financial default risk?
4. How can we identify patients likely to need additional support?

Note: These questions can be used to guide the development of machine learning models and statistical analyses using the treatment_journey_ml dataset. Each question may require different feature engineering approaches and modeling techniques.
